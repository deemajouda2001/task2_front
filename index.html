Gaza City

Gaza City is the largest city in the Gaza Strip, a narrow piece of land along the Mediterranean Sea in the Middle East.
It is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with a history dating back over 4,000 years.
The city is known for its cultural, historical, and economic significance, but it also faces significant challenges due to ongoing conflicts and blockades.
Despite this, Gaza City remains a vibrant hub of Palestinian life, with bustling markets, ancient landmarks, and a strong, resilient community.
